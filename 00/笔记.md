## PuNAC100-like/PubHLH61 co-regulate PuLOX3 mediating ester biosynthesis in cold-stored ‘Nanguo’ pears

## PuNAC100-like/PubHLH61共同调控PuLOX3介导冷藏“南国”梨中的酯的生物合成

本研究鉴定了与冷藏‘南国’梨酯损失密切相关的关键酶基因和转录因子。在冷藏的“南国”梨中，PuLOX3、PuNAC100-like 和 PubHLH61 的表达显着受到抑制，并且伴随着香气的消失。 PuNAC100-like 或 PubHLH61 的过度表达或沉默导致 PuLOX3 的过度表达或抑制。
我们分析了低温对酯类和关键基因PuLOX3的影响，并研究了[[转录因子]]，特别是NAC和bHLH转录因子如何调节酯类生物合成。通过生物信息学分析对转录因子进行分子表征，生化实验结果表明PuNAC100-like在体内/体外直接与PuLOX3启动子结合，从而影响酯的合成。我们提出了‘南果’梨酯类积累调控的模型。

酵母杂交（Yeast-hybridization）、电泳迁移率变动分析（electrophoretic mobility shift assay）和瞬时转染分析（transient transfection analyses）表明核定位的 PuNAC100-like 成功直接结合 PuLOX3 启动子，激活其表达。

[[酵母双杂交]]（Yeast two-hybrid）和[[双分子荧光互补]](bimolecular fluorescence complementation)表明PuNAC100-like与PubHLH61相互作用。此外，还观察到PubHLH61对PuNAC100-like的转录调控(transcriptional regulation)。

总的来说，这些研究结果表明，在低温胁迫下，PuNAC100-like 和 PubHLH61 的表达受到抑制，PuLOX3 的转录下调，从而导致冷藏‘南国’梨的香气品质下降。

In general, esters predominantly originate from the fatty acid metabolism pathway, which comprises two processes: I) β-oxidation (Schaffer et al, 2007), whereby alcohol dehydrogenase (ADH) and AAT convert acids and aldehydes into corresponding alcohols and then esterify them into esters (Zhu et al, 2020; Facundo et al, 2012), and II) the lipoxygenase pathway (LOX pathway), which involves four enzymes, including LOX, hydroperoxide lyase (HPL), ADH, and AAT. The substrates (mainly linoleic acid and linolenic acid) are oxidized and decomposed by LOXand HPL to produce aldehydes, ADH further reduces them to the cor-responding alcohols. Finally, AAT transfers the acyl group to the alcohol substrate, yielding ester substances (chaffer et al., 2007; Schwab et al., 2008; Schille et al., 2015). LOX is the first enzyme in these catalytic steps and has been proven to control the synthesis of esters (Schaffer et al., 
2007; Zhang et al., 2009; Schiller et al., 2015). For example, in 
cold-stored peaches, the loss of an ester was due to the lower activity of 
LOX and the decreased expression of PpLOX1 and PpLOX3 (Xi et al., 
2012; Zhang et al., 2011). Esters originating from the LOX pathway are 
the characteristic aromatic components of ripe ‘Nanguo’ pears (Zhou 
et al., 2015; Luo et al., 2021a, b). Research has demonstrated that cold 
storage results in a conspicuous decline in total ester content (Shi et al., 
2018a). Zhou et al. (2015) and Luo et al. (2021b) discovered that LOX 
contribute to ester biosynthesis in pears, suggesting that the production 
of esters was influenced by the expression of PuLOXs. 

一般来说，酯主要来源于脂肪酸代谢途径，该途径包括两个过程：I) β-氧化，其中醇脱氢酶(ADH)和AAT将酸和醛转化为相应的醇，然后将它们酯化II）脂氧合酶途径（LOX途径），涉及四种酶，包括LOX、氢过氧化物裂解酶（HPL）、ADH和AAT。底物（主要是亚油酸和亚麻酸）被LOX和HPL氧化分解生成醛，ADH进一步将其还原为相应的醇。最后，AAT将酰基转移到醇底物上，产生酯类物质。
[[LOX]]是这些催化步骤中的第一个酶，并已被证明可以控制酯的合成


## 结论
PuNAC100-like 可以通过直接结合 PuLOX3 的启动子来显着激活 PuLOX3 的表达，在酯的形成中充当正调节因子。 PubHLH61与PuNAC100-like之间存在相互作用，其中PubHLH61正向调节PuNAC100-like的表达水平，表现出酯生物合成关键基因复杂的TF调控网络。
